The Project

We have been working alongside the awesome Onefootball team for a while on several projects (see Onefootball Tablet and Onefootball Brasil). For this project we were asked to help craft a Material Design experience alongside numerous app improvements.

As one of the earlier Material Design implementations, an obvious challenge was working with a draft specification for Material Design. In these situations it really helps to be close to the design conversations coming out of Google.

Taming Transitions

An early concern of ours was that the over-use of transitions in some early Material Design implementations that we had seen. We feel the new Onefootball app demonstrates how the measured use of transitions can really improve the app experience.
