Manage HP Printers from Android Devices

The App

The HP SureSupply mobile app allows you to order printer ink and toner directly from your mobile device. The app can automatically monitor ink levels, and when cartridges run low you can order replacement cartridges from HP or other major retail stores.

The Project

We were brought in by the design agency craft the HP Sure Supply mobile experience. We were asked to bring our deep knowledge of Android to the project and lead the technical implementation.

As part of the implementation we implemented some of the core printer discovery and device recognition features, on top of which we created the set-up UI flows.

Finally, we were asked to advise on supporting many device screen sizes and densities as the app was rolled-out to include tablet support.
